Our comprehensive rental property inspection services cover a detailed photographic assessment of your Perth property. We provide room-by-room condition checks, verify smoke alarm and RCD compliance, assess visible gutter and fascia integrity, and scan for signs of damp or water leaks.

A PCR (Property Condition Report) is a mandatory legal document that records the property's condition at the start or end of a tenancy. Our PCR inspection services create a definitive record to protect against bond disputes. Routine inspections are periodic maintenance checks that focus on tenant care and identifying preventative repairs.

Most rental property inspection services are conducted by general administrative staff. At Site & Strata, our background in building maintenance means we have a trained eye for "early warning signs"—such as subtle moisture staining, foundation hairline cracks, or gutter sagging. While our inspections are visual and non-invasive, we provide a more proactive assessment of your property's physical health.

In Western Australia, a new PCR is required at the commencement of every new tenancy. Our PCR inspection services ensure that your documentation is always up to date with current REIWA standards and local Perth rental regulations, ensuring total compliance for property managers.
