Our Story & Commitment

Site & Strata Solutions WA Pty Ltd is a family-owned business established in 2010. Operating under the entity name **Site & Strata Solutions WA**, we provide reliable building maintenance and renovation services across the Perth region and Western Australia. Our trade-qualified team delivers high-quality repairs, installations, painting, and property upgrades with integrity and care.

As we've grown, we expanded our services to include professional rental inspections, supporting property managers and landlords with high-resolution reporting. As a registered NDIS provider, we are committed to inclusive home modifications and providing 24-hour emergency support.
